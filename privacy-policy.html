Privacy Policy

JD Keyword Extractor respects user privacy and is committed to protecting user data.

Information Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user information.

How the Extension Works

The extension analyzes job description content directly on the active webpage only when the user activates the extension. All processing happens locally within the user's browser.

Data Usage

No personal data, browsing history, authentication data, or identifiable information is collected or transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use third-party analytics, tracking tools, or external data collection services.

Permissions Usage

Permissions such as activeTab and scripting are used solely to extract job description keywords from the currently active webpage after user interaction.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future to reflect improvements or feature updates.
